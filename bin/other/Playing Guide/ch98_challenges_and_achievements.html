Chapter 98: Challenges and Achievements

This chapter is devoted to giving suggestions of interesting challenges to vary up the game if you get bored playing the game normally. They are grouped roughly by their difficulty. A number indicated in parenthesis is a point value. While the game will not track any of this information for you (as of right now), you can sum up all the point values for challenges that you've completed in a game to get an achievement score. How high of a score can you achieve?

Note that the difficulty groupings are based on the difficulty of the challenge and do not mandate playing at a specific global difficulty.

There are no guarantees that any of these challenges are doable. If you believe a challenge is impossible, consider sending the game's author an email; however, there are no guarantees that a change will be made.

Novice Challenges:

Maze Runner (3)

Using walls and towers, force the enemy to travel through a large portion of the map, and complete your walls by level 30.

For scoring purposes, you must do all of the following:

  • Make a path surrounded by walls/towers for both ground and air troops.
  • Attempt to use space as efficiently as possible. In other words, there should be as few gaps in the map (besides those for the enemy's path) as possible.
  • The above two conditions must be met in full before the start of level 30.
Tower Synergy (3)

Buy at least one of each tower during the game.

For scoring purposes, you must do all of the following:

  • Buy one of each tower. The towers must be on the map at the game's conclusion.
  • For the purposes of this challenge, the "Spray Tower NE" and "Spray Tower SE" count as the same tower.
  • Walls count for this challenge.
Cheap Investment (5)

Fully upgrade a "Cheap Tower" and keep it until the end of the game.

For scoring purposes, you must do all of the following:

  • Buy a "Cheap Tower".
  • Fully upgrade it to max level (10 at the time of writing this) and keep the tower until the end of the game.
Unlock Custom Games (15)

Unlock the ability to play custom games by reaching level 100. As an additional bonus, the level box will be outlined in bronze instead of the typical gray starting at level 100.

For scoring purposes, you must do all of the following:

  • Reach the end of level 99 with at least one health.

Intermediate Challenges

Flawless Victory (25)

Reach level 100 without losing any lives.

For scoring purposes, you must do all of the following:

  • Never buy health at any time during the game before level 100.
  • Reach the end of level 99 with 20 life remaining.
  • This restriction only applies until the end of level 99.

Advanced Challenges

Upgrade Maniac (40)

Fully upgrade each bought tower before buying a new one.

For scoring purposes, you must do all of the following:

  • Before buying a new tower, all already-placed towers must be upgraded.
  • You must survive to the end of level 99 to score any points.
  • This restriction applies for the entire game.

Expert Challenges

Who Needs Walls? (75)

Reach level 100 without buying a single wall. This challenge applies for the entire game up to the stated level.

For scoring purposes, you must do all of the following:

  • Never buy a wall during the course of the game.
  • This restriction only applies until the end of level 99.
