System Requirements:

Minimum:
Operating System: Microsoft Windows 7 or later.
Screen Resolution: 1024x768 or higher.
RAM: 32 MB (of free memory)

Recommended:
Operating System: Microsoft Windows 7 or later.
Screen Resolution: 1024x768 or higher.
RAM: 64 MB (of free memory)
CPU: My computer possesses an Intel(R) Core(TM) i5-6200 CPU @ 2.30 GHz 2.40 GHz; you could probably get by with way lower speeds though.
