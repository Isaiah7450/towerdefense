System Requirements:

Minimum:

Operating System: Microsoft Windows 7 or later.
Screen Resolution: 1024x768 or higher.
RAM: 256 MB (of free memory)
CPU: Anything made sinc 2015 should work.
Graphics Card: Anything made since 2015 should work.
Peripherals: A mouse and keyboard.

Recommended:

Operating System: Microsoft Windows 7 or later.
Screen Resolution: 1440x900 or higher.
RAM: 512 MB (of free memory)
CPU: Intel(R) Core(TM) i5-6200 CPU @ 2.30 GHz 2.40 GHz or better.
Graphics Card: Anything made since 2015 should work.
Peripherals: A mouse and keyboard.
